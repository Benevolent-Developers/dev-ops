Vision Statement

Motivation/Opportunity


According to NYS DMV "Summary of Alcohol-Related Motor Vehicles" the report states 7,849 total crashes, 270 resulting fatal crashes. In addition to " Summary of Drug-Related Motor Vehicle Crashes " report states a total of 1,239 total crashes, 170 resulting in fatal crashes, all in 2014.
We believe that this is a concern in mordern society that needs addressing. Methods have been taken by NYS to rehabilitate, programs have been introduced to help individuals with substance abuse. Despite positive outcomes, our team believes that there's more that can be done. The EAC Network provides interesting material regarding Leandra's Law.

Problem


The problem of our client is to inform Leandra's law which now affects most of our client's clients, the impact of which is clients are not properly informed of subjective or trivial changes of Leandra's law including information on Ignition Interlock. A successfull solution would be to create a " Content Management System " combined with " Responsive Design."

Product Position


Our goal is to ultimately design a " Content Management System " for Suffolk County TASC in a group of four programmers, our client's client will receive the latest information on Ignition Interlock and Leandra's law. Our system is divided into two logical parts: front-end and back-end. The front-end compartment of our system, work such that it provides a friendly user interface for our client to dynamically add/delete/update posts and pages, and our back-end interface interacts with the database, additionally providing computation.
Unlike existing CMSs e.g.   Wordpress, Joomla, and Drupal. Our product offers an exclusive state of the art front-end and back-end logical components written by experienced software developers.

Product Users


The users of our product will be the client — for managing content — and our client's client that will be informed and updated on Leandra's law and Ignition Interlock device usage. We expect our users to learn from a familiar user interface.

Feature List


  • Back-End
    Part of a software that handles all database interaction, additionally providing calculation
  • Content Management System
    A software fundamentally divided into two parts: back-end and front-end. Allowing the software user to manage content from a website
  • Front-End
    Part of a software that handles all user interaction e.g user interface, control panel design
  • Responsive Design
    Way to design a website such that it is compatible with mobile devices, offering accessibility as well
